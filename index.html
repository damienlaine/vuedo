Daily Routines Don\'t Have to Be Stressful

For many neuro-diverse children, transitions and daily tasks can be overwhelming. Visual supports have been proven to reduce anxiety, improve independence, and create predictable, comfortable routines.

Predictable & Visual

Clear visual schedules help children know what to expect, reducing anxiety around transitions and new activities.

Engaging & Personalized

AI-generated comic strips featuring your child\'s avatar make routines fun and relatable, increasing motivation and participation.

Builds Independence

Step-by-step visual guidance empowers children to complete tasks independently, building confidence and life skills.

Designed for Neuro-Diverse Needs

Every feature is carefully crafted with input from research, families, and professionals working with neuro-diverse children.

01

AI-Powered Personalization

Transform everyday activities into custom comic strips featuring your child\'s avatar. Choose your preferred visual style and watch routines come to life in a format your child connects with.

02

Focused Kiosk Mode

Fullscreen, distraction-free interface designed for children. Large buttons, clear visual feedback, and simple gestures make interaction intuitive and stress-free.

03

Visual Timers & Rewards

Clear visual timers help children understand time passing. Celebrate each completed step with engaging visual rewards like confetti and sparkles to reinforce positive behavior.

04

Works Offline

Once routines are created, they work perfectly offline. No internet needed for daily use, ensuring consistent access without connectivity concerns.

05

Privacy-First Design

Your child\'s photos are never stored. We only keep the AI-generated avatars and style preferences. Full GDPR compliance with transparent data practices and parental controls.

06

Neuro-Inclusive Interface

Designed following WCAG 2.1 AA standards with high contrast, clear typography, error-tolerant interactions, and no audio elements that could be overwhelming.

How Vue&Do Works

Getting started with Vue&Do is simple. Create personalized routines in minutes.

1

Create Your Household

Add family members, caregivers, and professionals. Optionally upload photos to generate personalized avatars in your preferred comic style.

2

Build Visual Routines

Choose from templates or create custom routines. Add steps with clear icons, labels, and optional timers. Each step becomes a panel in the daily comic strip.

3

Child Uses Kiosk Mode

Your child follows the routine in a simple, fullscreen interface. Large "Doing" and "Done" buttons with visual rewards celebrate each completed step.

4

Celebrate & Build Skills

Watch as your child gains confidence and independence. Adjust routines as needed and celebrate progress together with engaging visual feedback.
